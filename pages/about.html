About Us

Your one-stop for premium hot tub covers, made in-house for 25 years.

REPLACEMENT HOT TUB SPA COVERS

Lillypad Cover Company Ltd. is a family business with a 25 year background in vinyl fabrication.

Over the years we have made inflatable air domes for swimming pools, liners, lifting balloons for ocean salvage, awnings, etc. Since the early to mid nineties our focus has been Hot Tub Covers.

We were one of the first to successfully go Factory Direct to the consumer (in our area). Our past experience and current prices are based on the lowest we can charge without affecting quality in materials, labour or service.

All covers are tapered to shed water. Polyester coated vinyl on under-side (not plastic). Optional reflective material otherwise.

Your source for premium, factory-direct hot tub covers since 1996.
